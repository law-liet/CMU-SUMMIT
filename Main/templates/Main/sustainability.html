Sustainability Panel

Panel Description

Energy and Sustainability Panel is one of the five major panels of CMU Summit. This event is featured with keynote speech, panel discussion and Q&A session. We aim to give CMU students insight about the cutting-edge technological developments in energy and sustainability, help students of interest establish better understanding of sustainable energy industries, and become prepared for their professional life. We promise an open space for academic talks, experience sharing, and collision of ideas.

Topics

  • Response of conventional energy industry to the regulation and market changes
  • Potential replacements of fossil fuel and the technical challenges
  • Commercial and residential energy efficiency and smart house
